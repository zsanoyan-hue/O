Who We Are

Racing Toward
the Stars

Team Astra is a STEM Racing (formerly F1 in Schools) team competing in the Development Class. We design, manufacture, and brand a miniature CO₂-powered racing car using professional CAD/CAM tools and real engineering methodology.

Our name reflects our drive — just as stars are formed under pressure, we thrive on challenge, precision, and the pursuit of excellence at every competition level.

Design Philosophy

Inspired by celestial bodies — our car's profile echoes the elongated form of a comet, with swept surfaces promoting laminar airflow and minimal pressure drag.

Livery & Branding

Deep navy and gold — the colours of the night sky and starlight. The Astra spike-star emblem appears prominently on the sidepods, unified with our team identity.

Halo System

Incorporating the 2025 regulation Halo deceleration structure, engineered with optimal placement to maintain weight distribution and comply fully with the updated STEM Racing Technical Regulations.

Sustainability Commitment

Team Astra is committed to sustainable engineering practices. Our manufacturing process minimises material waste through optimised nesting strategies, offcuts are repurposed for testing, and we offset our team's travel footprint. The Astra Mk.I was designed with a full lifecycle review, from raw material sourcing to end-of-life recyclability.

The Season

Our Journey

From first concept sketch to competition day — a timeline of how Team Astra brought the Mk.I to life.

Phase 01 — Kickoff
Team Formation & Research
Roles assigned, STEM Racing regulations studied in depth, competitive benchmarking of past portfolios completed. Initial design brief established with performance targets and brand direction.
Phase 02 — Design
CAD Modelling & Analysis
Multiple concept iterations developed in Autodesk Inventor. Virtual wind tunnel analysis conducted to evaluate and refine the car's aerodynamic form. Final design locked after three rounds of revision.
Phase 03 — Manufacturing
CNC Machining & Finishing
CAM toolpaths generated and validated. CNC machining completed with precision tolerances. Extensive post-processing: sanding from 120 to 2000 grit, priming, and multi-coat livery painting.
Phase 04 — Branding
Livery, Pit Display & Portfolios
Team brand finalised. Car livery applied. Pit display designed and printed. Engineering and enterprise portfolios written, designed, and reviewed against STEM Racing judging criteria.
Phase 05 — Competition
Regional Finals & Beyond
Astra competes at the Regional Finals — presenting portfolios, undergoing scrutineering, racing head-to-head, and delivering verbal presentations to judging panels. Eyes set on the National Finals.
